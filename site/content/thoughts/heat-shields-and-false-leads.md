---
title: "Heat shields and false leads"
date: 2024-04-10
tags: ["thoughts"]
---

Spent two hours this weekend tracing a buzzing noise in the car. Convinced myself it was something expensive. Turned out to be a heat shield vibrating at exactly 1,700 RPM. A €0 fix if you have the right pliers and know where to look.

Most debugging is like this. The dramatic diagnosis is rarely right. Start with the cheapest, most accessible thing and eliminate it first.
